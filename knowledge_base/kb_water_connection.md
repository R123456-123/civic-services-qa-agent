# New Water Connection — Indore Municipal Corporation

## Overview
Any property in Indore that is not yet connected to the municipal water supply can apply for a new connection through the IMC website, which links to the MP e-Nagarpalika portal for processing.

## How to apply
1. Go to the IMC official website and select "Pay Your Bills & Tax Online."
2. Under "Water Charges," choose "New Application."
3. Select your city and enter the required property and applicant details.
4. Search to confirm your property record, then submit the application along with scanned supporting documents through the e-Nagarpalika portal.
5. The system generates a Connection Number and routes the application through an internal approval workflow.
6. You can track your application status at any stage. If it is rejected, the portal shows the rejection remark and you can reapply.

## Documents typically needed
- Proof of property ownership or occupancy
- Applicant's ID proof
- Address proof
(Exact document list is confirmed on the portal at the time of application — it can vary slightly by zone.)

## Fees
Connection charges depend on the connection/pipe size and zone, and are displayed during the application process before payment.

## Paying an existing water bill
Water bills can be paid online through the IMC portal (search by Consumer ID, name, ward, and zone) or through UPI apps such as PhonePe, Google Pay, and Paytm.

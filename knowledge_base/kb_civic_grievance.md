# Civic Complaints & Grievance Redressal — Indore

## Overview
Indore has a layered complaint system: a fast first-response channel (Indore 311 app) run by IMC, and a state-level escalation channel (MP CM Helpline) for unresolved issues.

## Filing a complaint — Indore 311 app
1. Open the Indore 311 app (available on Android and iOS) and register/log in.
2. Select "Register new complaint." The app automatically detects your location.
3. Choose the relevant category: Garbage, Road & Footpath, Public Toilets/Urinals, Park & Playground, Water Supply, Sewerage/Drainage, Public Health, Street Light, or Other.
4. Describe the issue and attach a photo, then submit.
5. You receive a complaint/reference number to track resolution. IMC's internal target is same-day to 24-hour resolution for many categories.

## If the complaint isn't resolved
1. Escalate to IMC's Public Grievance Cell (Deputy Commissioner's or Mayor's office).
2. If still unresolved, call the Madhya Pradesh CM Helpline at 181, or message WhatsApp +91-755-2555582.
3. You can also lodge a grievance through the MP Jan Seva (CM Helpline) online portal, which escalates to the State Appellate Authority under the Department of Urban Administration & Development.

## Other services on the same complaint infrastructure
The e-Nagarpalika portal's grievance form can also be used for civic complaints tied to a specific municipal service (water, sewerage, licensing, etc.), as an alternative to the 311 app.

# Death Certificate — Madhya Pradesh / Indore

## Legal requirement
A death must be reported and registered within 21 days of occurrence, similar to birth registration.

## How to apply
1. Go to the MP e-Nagarpalika portal (or the CRS portal) and select Death Certificate under Birth & Death Registration.
2. Enter the deceased's name, date and place of death, and the applicant's details and relationship to the deceased.
3. Upload the required documents: hospital death report or cremation/burial slip, and ID/address proof of the applicant.
4. Submit the application and pay the fee if applicable — this generates a registration/acknowledgement number.
5. The local Registrar or Municipal Health Department verifies the details, typically within about 5 working days.
6. Once verified, download the certificate from the portal or collect a printed copy from the municipal/ward office.

## Downloading an existing certificate
Select "Download Certificate," enter the registration number, name of the deceased, and date of death, then search and download the PDF.

## Why it matters
A death certificate is needed for property inheritance claims, closing bank accounts, insurance settlements, and other legal processes.
